the project requirements broken down by the report and the presentation:

**For Report Structure**
The report should follow this specific order and format:

1.  **Abstract**
2.  **Introduction**
3.  **Related Works**
4.  **Methods**
5.  **Experimental Results**
6.  **Conclusion**
7.  **Reference**

- **Format Constraints:**
  - The entire report must be **no less than 12 pages**.
  - It must be formatted in a **Double Column** layout.

the report should be formatted as IEEE conference format, double column, no less than 12 pages, including references

---

**For Presentation**
The presentation should cover the following points:

1.  **Overview** – Tell the audience what you have done.
2.  **Model Structure**
3.  **Experimental Results**
4.  **Conclusion** – This section must include:
    - Strength
    - Weakness
    - Future Work

- **Format Constraints:**
  - The presentation must be **no more than 5 slides**.
